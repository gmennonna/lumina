Bring this to your session

This timeline is a starting point, not an ending point.

Lumina can surface patterns and open questions, but the real work happens in conversation — ideally with a trained therapist who can hold space for both of you. Consider bringing this timeline to your next couples session.

What to share
Show your therapist the merged timeline and Lumina's analysis. Let them guide the conversation — don't read it out loud to each other without support.
If you don't have a therapist
Use the conversation starters from Lumina's analysis in a calm, private moment. One question at a time. Listen more than you speak.
Lumina is not a substitute for licensed therapy. If you're in crisis, call 988 or text HOME to 741741.
